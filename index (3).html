About

Who I am

I am a Civil Servant based in London with over 20 years of professional experience, mainly in the public sector. My day-to-day work includes community projects, workplace safety, logistics, and practical IT/admin support. I enjoy solving real problems, supporting teams, and making complex tasks simpler and safer.

I completed an MBA in International Business (Distinction) and a BSc in Cloud Computing (First Class) at the University of Wales Trinity Saint David in London. My academic work includes a business development dissertation on nearshore IT support for UK organisations and an earlier thesis and viva presentation on a mobile application concept to help address Violence Against Women.

Today my focus is a combination of business management, public sector operations, digital tools and safety. I am interested in how these areas connect inside organisations and how they can be improved step by step, using data, reflection, and practical experience. In the future, I plan to continue this path at doctoral level.

Projects & case studies

MBA dissertation
Nearshore IT Support for UK Organisations

Business development study on how UK SMEs can benefit from nearshore IT teams in Central and Eastern Europe, especially Hungary. The work looks at service levels, communication, culture, and risk from a practical point of view.

#InternationalBusiness #NearshoreIT #SMEs
BSc research
Violence Against Women (VAW) Support App

A mobile app concept designed to support individuals at risk of Violence Against Women, focusing on accessible information, emergency pathways, and privacy. Presented in dissertation, viva and research poster formats.

#SocialImpact #MobileApp #UX
Cloud project
Cloud Computing in Practice

Undergraduate work on how organisations can adopt cloud services in a safe and effective way, including security, scalability and reliability aspects, and a practical migration concept for a real-world style case.

#Cloud #Security #Architecture
Business concept

A concept brand and operating model for Level 1–3 IT support delivered from Hungary to UK clients. Developed as a structured business proposal with market analysis, service design and financial outline.
